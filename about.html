About Live NYC

Live NYC is NYC’s official map of every place on every block — built to help New Yorkers and visitors discover local businesses, public spaces, and citywide moments in real time.

This site is a curated gateway into the Live XYZ map library, with collections organized by country, neighborhood/corridor, and category.

Partners

Live NYC is powered by partnerships across NYC government, civic organizations, and local business communities.
