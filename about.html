About Live NYC Map

Live NYC Map is a curated library of maps and guides built on Live XYZ’s citywide data. Our goal is simple: help New Yorkers and visitors discover great places across all five boroughs — and make it easier to explore small businesses, neighborhoods, and events in real time.

What you’ll find here

A fast, lightweight way to browse Live XYZ’s map library — by country, neighborhood/corridor, and special events. Each map opens in a new tab so you can keep browsing the library while exploring.

Partners

Live XYZ works with public and civic partners across New York City to support local discovery and small business visibility.

Maps open on share.livexyz.com. This site is a lightweight, shareable library for exploring Live NYC Map content.
