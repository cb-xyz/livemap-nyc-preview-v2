About Live NYC

Live NYC is powered by Live XYZ — a local places technology company supporting NYC with ground-truth data on storefronts, public-facing places, and the public realm. We’re building a living map of New York to help people discover what’s open, what’s happening, and what makes the city feel alive.

A living map of the city
This site is a public-facing layer of curated maps and guides — designed for Summer 2026 and built to grow with NYC. If you’re a business, organization, or local partner and want your place represented accurately, we’d love to hear from you.
